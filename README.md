# 🌸 Saha.ai 🌸

> **Your mind, beautifully mapped.**

Saha.ai is a proactive well-being platform designed to bridge the gap between daily self-reflection and actionable emotional intelligence. By writing naturally in a digital journal, users receive deep AI-driven insights into their mood, helping them identify hidden patterns and access personalized resources to recover, reset, and grow.

---

## ✨ Key Features

*   **Natural Journaling:** A seamless, distraction-free space to record your daily thoughts.
*   **Deep Emotional Insights:** Translates raw journal entries into meaningful emotional data, mapping out your mental well-being over time.
*   **Interactive Dashboard & Growth Tracking:** Monitor your mood trends, view your **Mood Calendar**, and track your personal development streaks.
*   **Personal Library & Recovery Resources:** Actionable insights paired with tailored content to help you navigate stressful days and support emotional recovery.
*   **Emergency SOS Support:** Instant access to 24/7 crisis helplines, emergency contacts, and professional counseling resources when immediate help is needed.
